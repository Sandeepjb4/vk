🔒 Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains how we collect and use your data.

1. Data Collection

We collect your name, phone number, location, property/job preferences, and any message you submit through our forms or WhatsApp link.

2. Purpose of Data

We use the data to connect you with the right accommodation, job, or service. We may also use it to follow up with you and improve our service.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely and only accessible by VK International LV management. We do not share your data with third parties except as required by law.

4. WhatsApp Communication

By submitting a form or clicking our WhatsApp link, you agree to be contacted via WhatsApp. You can request to stop communication anytime.

5. Data Retention

We retain your data only for as long as needed to fulfill our service. You can request deletion of your data by contacting us.

6. Security Measures

We implement strict data protection measures and access restrictions. Our team is trained to handle personal data responsibly and ethically.

7. Updates to Policy

This privacy policy may be updated without prior notice. Changes will be reflected on this page.
